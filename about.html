ABOUT US

Our Desgin Tale

Established in 2000, Modern furniture Pvt. Ltd. is one of India’s foremost design-led, interior fit-out brands, with over 20 lakh sq. ft of luxury interior space executed for high-end hospitality and residential spaces. Our roster of clients includes hospitality majors like The Sheraton, Novotel, Dunes, Samroha, Saptha Hospitality, Holiday Group, Ananta Living, Gokulam Hotels & Resorts, etc. We do projects that include corporates, high-profile individuals, and celebrities.

Our folio of contract assignments also includes high-end contract grade furniture involving over 20,000 unique pieces using over 3,00,000 cu. ft of wood, also working with special materials like stainless steel, wrought iron, bronze, leather, wicker, and jute.

Vision

Modern Furniture was a solution to the gripping issue, the furniture industry was facing at one point. The unavailability of quality furniture, lack of innovative designs, and scarcity of premium quality raw materials. The workmanship was unorganized. China and Indonesia had become the furniture point for middle and upper-class furniture dealers. These pieces of furniture had to withstand the humid and hot weather conditions for which it was not made, questioning it’s make and durability.

Modern Furniture started the journey to create a local market for Indian-made designer furniture that will reduce the import of furniture from countries like China and Indonesia. Dtale Modern advocates the ‘Make in India’ initiative and thus want every piece of furniture to be a combination of the new design, skilled craftsmanship, premium quality wood, and other materials. The vision was to bring together skilled craftsmanship and premium quality raw materials under one roof and that we did.

Mission

Modern Furniture Art Studio is an experimentation floor for it’s artisans. With handcrafted furniture, art, and accessories combined with innovative designs and highly skilled artisanship,Modern Furniture envisions to be the patron of premium quality designer furniture and accessories. The ‘Make in India’ initiative is highly valued at Dtale Modern and the artisans are treasured here for their creativity and talent. Nurturing the creative and financial aspirations of artisans is the primary mission of Dtale Modern for only a free mind can create magic with mind and hands.
